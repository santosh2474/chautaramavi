Course Detail

School(Class:Nursery to 10)

Nursery to class 10 is the foundational stage of a child's education where they learn basic skills and knowledge that form the basis for their future academic and personal development.

During this period, children are exposed to a variety of subjects such as mathematics, science, social studies, language arts, and more. They also develop social skills, emotional intelligence, and physical abilities through extracurricular activities such as sports, music, and art.

Chautara Mavi is an educational institution that offers comprehensive and holistic education for students from nursery to class 10. The academy focuses on nurturing each child's unique abilities and interests while ensuring they acquire the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed academically and socially.

Chautara Mavi provides a student-centered learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. The academy's curriculum is designed to be engaging and interactive, using a variety of teaching methods and technologies to enhance learning outcomes.

In addition to academic excellence, Chautara Mavi places great emphasis on character development and social responsibility. The academy encourages students to develop a strong sense of ethics, empathy, and respect for diversity. They also engage in various community service projects to help them develop a sense of responsibility towards society.

Overall, the nursery to class 10 stage of education is a crucial period in a child's life, and institutions like Chautara Mavi play a vital role in shaping their future. With their focus on academic excellence, character development, and social responsibility, Chautara Mavi is well-positioned to prepare students for success in all aspects of life.

Chautara Mavi is a school that follows the Montessori method of education. It provides a child-centered approach to learning that encourages children to learn at their own pace and in their own way. The school emphasizes the development of independence, self-discipline, and a love of learning in its students. Chautara Mavi offers a range of Montessori programs for children from the age of 2.5 to 6 years old.
